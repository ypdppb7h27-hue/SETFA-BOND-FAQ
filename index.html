Funding & Financial Transparency
How much will this bond cost property owners?

The proposed rate is $0.22 per $1,000 of assessed value. For example, a $500,000 assessed property would pay approximately $110 per year. Final amounts are determined by Thurston County based on official assessed value.

How long would the bond last?

The proposed bond would be structured for approximately 25 years.

Can bond funds be used for staffing or wages?

No. Bond funds may only be used for capital improvements such as stations and apparatus. Staffing and wages are funded separately through the annual levy operating budget.

Facilities & Apparatus
Why replace fire engines?

Three fire engines are aging (34, 29, and 21 years old). Modern safety standards, reliability concerns, and response readiness require replacement.

Why renovate instead of build new stations?

Renovation provides updated facilities at a lower cost than new construction while addressing safety, response efficiency, and firefighter health standards.

Community Impact
Will this improve response times?

Yes. Strategic renovations and apparatus updates improve coverage distribution and reduce turnout delays.

Will insurance rates be affected?

Modernized facilities and apparatus can help maintain or improve ISO ratings, which may influence homeowner insurance premiums.
